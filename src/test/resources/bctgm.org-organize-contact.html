CONTACT A BCTGM ORGANIZER

If you and your coworkers are interested in learning more about organizing your workplace, contact us by filling out this form. A BCTGM organizer in your region will contact you and set up a meeting with you and your co-workers.

 

All of the information you provide is kept 100% confidential. Please provide only your personal or home e-mail address and phone number.

 

Please note: only inquiries about organizing will be met with a response. For all other inquiries, please contact the International Union in writing or by telephone.

Welcome! I write two mystery series for Penguin Random House Books: the Bibliophile Mysteries starring bookbinder and rare book expert Brooklyn Wainwright, and the Fixer-Upper Mysteries, featuring contractor and Victorian home expert, Shannon Hammer. Three of the Fixer-Upper Mysteries have been brought to the small screen by Hallmark Movies & Mysteries. You can read free excerpts of all of my books. Get started on the Books page.

While you’re here, please be sure to sign up for my Newsletter. Members of my Newsletter List get access to The Secret Room where you’ll find extra treats like puzzles and contests, exclusive excerpts, cast of characters rundowns, photos, recipes, and more! Plus—and most-importantly—I’ll send you an email when I release a new book.

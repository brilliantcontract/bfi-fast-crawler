The Alaska Airmen's Association

Protecting, Preserving, and Promoting General Aviation since 1951

A VOICE FOR YOU

At the Alaska Airmen's Association, we're more than just a community; we're a vibrant, passion-driven movement dedicated to safeguarding, preserving, and advancing General Aviation in Alaska. With over 2,000 members, we represent one of the largest and most dynamic aviation communities in the nation, a testament to Alaska's high number of licensed pilots per capita.

 

Our mission is to unite experienced aviators and aviation enthusiasts in a shared commitment to enhance safety, promote aviation, and support initiatives that benefit our growing aviation community. This strength enables us to advocate effectively for access and safety infrastructure across the state.

Whether you're a seasoned pilot or just beginning your aviation journey, the Alaska Airmen's Association is your gateway to exploring the wonders of flight in the Last Frontier. Join us as we navigate the skies, championing the spirit of adventure and camaraderie that defines Alaska's aviation legacy. Share your feedback with our Government and Legislative Affairs Advocate to help guide our efforts and ensure we continue to serve the needs of our aviation community.

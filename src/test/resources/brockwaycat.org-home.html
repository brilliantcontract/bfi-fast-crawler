Get the Skills for a Rewarding Career in Healthcare

No-Cost Healthcare Career Training in Brockway, PA and Titusville, PA

View Programs     Request Info

Career-Focused Healthcare Training That Takes Months – Not Years. All Offered at No Cost.

Are you looking to start training towards a career in healthcare? Since 2013, Brockway Center for Arts and Technology (BCAT) has been helping students gain the skills, hands-on training, and experience they need to pursue a rewarding healthcare career. At BCAT, we know how difficult it can be trying to make ends meet while pursuing your career goals. That’s why all our training programs are offered at no cost.  

We provide a fast career pathway to employment in the healthcare industry by offering a variety of programs ranging in length from 3 weeks to 9.5 months*. Located in Brockway, PA and Titusville, PA, you will feel right at home on our campus while training to become a healthcare professional.

Our Programs

  • Clinical Medical Assistant and Phlebotomy
    Clinical Medical Assistant and Phlebotomy

    Medical Assistants are indispensable for keeping a physician’s office, hospital, or clinic running smoothly. This program will also provide you with the skills needed to become a Phlebotomist. Learn more.

  • Pharmacy Technician
    Pharmacy Technician

    Pharmacy Technicians support Pharmacists in providing consumers with the best possible pharmaceutical care. Get the skills and training you need to start a professional pharmaceutical career in as little as 7 months*. Learn more.

What Sets Us Apart?

Career-Focused Training – Our programs are designed to provide the relevant, up-to-date skills employers are looking for in the growing healthcare industry. As a BCAT student, all of your time will be spent on relevant skills you’ll use on the job.

No-Cost Education – At Brockway Center for Arts and Technology, we believe in investing your mind, not your money. That’s why all programs are offered at no cost to students, allowing you to focus entirely on graduating with career-ready skills.

On-Site Experience – Each of our programs includes an externship. Whether your externship takes place at a hospital, doctor’s office, or pharmacy, you will get first-hand experience and training in the healthcare field, giving you a head start in a thriving industry.

Small Class Sizes – At BCAT, we deliberately keep class sizes small to ensure you get personalized, one-on-one support from your instructors. You’ll feel fully supported and able to gain the skills you need in less time through our small class model.

At Brockway Center for Arts and Technology, we know how difficult it can be trying to make ends meet while pursuing your career goals. That’s why all our programs are offered at no cost.

Tuition is not charged to students who are residents of Pennsylvania and also have their high school diploma or equivalent. Funding for Brockway Center for Arts and Technology is received through the state, foundations, grants, and individual donors. The tuition, fees, uniforms, scrubs, and textbooks are covered for all accepted students.

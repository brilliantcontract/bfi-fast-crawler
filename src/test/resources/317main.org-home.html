Where community finds connection.

For more than two decades, we’ve created spaces where self-confidence grows, friendships form, and music serves as a bridge between generations and cultures.

From lessons, ensembles, and concerts, to community outreach, access, and events, 317 Main is more than a music school. More than a venue. More than an intergenerational gathering place for children, teens, and adults. We are a cultural hub—a space for musical discovery and personal growth, both onsite at our downtown Yarmouth campus and throughout Maine.

That’s what we said at the House Party this year, and we meant it. In a time that’s tricky and hard, that truth rings louder than ever. 317 Main is more than a music center—it’s a gathering place where people of all ages come together and where music helps us navigate life’s transitions with hope and joy.

“We Belong To Each Other.”

YES! I support connection, joy, and belonging through music.

Ready to Make Music?

Whether you're picking up an instrument for the first time or looking to sharpen your skills, 317 Main has a place for you.

From one-on-one lessons to dynamic group ensembles, and hands-on workshops to our recording studio, our programs are designed to meet you where you are—and take you further. Learn at your own pace, connect with others, and grow as a musician in a vibrant, welcoming community.

Discover what you can do when you’re supported by great teachers and a love of music.

LITTLE ROOTS & EARLY EDUCATION

Playful, musical, and rooted in connection—Little Roots invites young children and their caregivers to explore rhythm, melody, and movement together. These joyful classes plant the seeds of lifelong musical discovery.

Learn more

PRIVATE & GROUP LESSONS

Learn at your own pace or play alongside others—our lessons offer personalized instruction in a supportive, community-centered environment. From first notes to advanced technique, we meet you where you are and help you grow.

Learn more

WRITE IT. PLAY IT. RECORD IT.

Bring your sound to life in the Crewe Recording Studio. With support for songwriting, arranging, and recording, it's a space where creativity takes center stage.

Learn more

PLAY WITH OTHERS, GROW TOGETHER.

From folk jams and rock bands to jazz sessions and vocal groups, our ensembles build confidence, stretch skills, and share the magic of making music in harmony.

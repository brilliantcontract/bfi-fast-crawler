Ive made up stories in my head since I can first remember. Everything I see, hear or read spurs more ideas.

B.J. Daniels was born in Texas but moved with her family to Montana at the age of five. Her first home was a cabin in the Gallatin Canyon and later a lake house on Hebgen Lake outside of West Yellowstone. Most of her books are set in Montana, a place she loves. 

I still get a thrill when I see one of my books on the shelf and I never take any of it for granted.

B.J. has written more than 130 books for both Harlequin Intrigue and HQN. She has won numerous awards and her books have appeared on The New York Times and USA Today Bestseller lists.

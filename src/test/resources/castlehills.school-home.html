Preschool

Elementary
The elementary years at Castle Hills provide a solid academic and spiritual foundation for life! Our Elementary School consists of grades Kindergarten through the fourth grade, with students advancing to our Intermediate School for fifth and sixth grades.

Intermediate School
Unique to the San Antonio area, our Intermediate School (fifth and sixth grade) provides a great stepping stone for that sometimes-difficult transition from elementary to middle school.

Middle School
Taught from a decidedly Christian worldview, the middle school program at Castle Hills offers a rigorous academic curriculum along with many elective opportunities. Skilled teachers provide comprehensive learning through small class sizes, advanced technology, and real-world application.

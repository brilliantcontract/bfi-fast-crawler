"Old Wichita Money" is how I have heard this club described on multiple occasions. A local legend known by many hidden off the beaten path and tucked away. Easy access off both Central and 13th this Oldie Goldie is upscale dining in a casual club atmosphere. Polite staff who will quickly learn your name and serve you your favorites from memory.

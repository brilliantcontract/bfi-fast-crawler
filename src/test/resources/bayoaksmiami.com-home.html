The Best of the Best Assisted Living

Bay Oaks is proud to be recognized as the very best in retirement and assisted living. We were voted Best of Miami-Dade 2023 by The Miami Herald. Our work had previously been championed on the front page of The Miami Herald, who lauded us as “the Miracle on 34th Street,” and “Gracious assisted living.” They recognized our ” healthy,… fortunate,… hopeful” covid-free residents in facing the pandemic. We have also been featured on CBS Miami news for innovative partnerships with local government that meant our residents were the first to be tested for the coronavirus in the City of Miami. We were also the reader’s choice for best retirement residence in the last Best of South Florida issue of the Miami SunPost and we are listed in the top Assisted Living Facilities by Assisted Living Magazine.

Of course, our most cherished accolades come directly from our residents and their families. When you tour Bay Oaks, be sure to see the archives of thank you letters, signed cards, and charitable endowments gifted to us by residents of today and yesteryear.

We also invite you read reviews onGoogle,Yelp, andCaring.com.

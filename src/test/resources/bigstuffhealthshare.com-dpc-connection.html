The Missing Piece in Your DPC Practice

We support direct primary care providers by helping your patients pay for the big stuff.

Book a call to learn more.

As a direct primary care provider, you’re a pioneer in the medical world. You make your patients feel seen and cared for. And when it comes to medical billing, you’re a bit of a rebel. 

You’ve stepped away from complex insurance billing so you can:

  • Focus on your personalized care

  • Make healthcare more transparent and affordable

  • Simplify your administrative work

  • Enjoy a fulfilling and lucrative career

DPC is a refreshing approach for patients and medical providers.

But something is missing…

“What about the big stuff?”

As a primary care provider, you can handle most everyday healthcare needs at affordable prices. But even healthy families worry about how to handle major medical expenses, like an unexpected stay or maternity care.

This can leads patients to turn to traditional health insurance, making it more difficult for DPC providers to attract and retain cash-pay clients.

That’s where we come in.

Teaming up for all-around affordable healthcare.

We help members of your DPC practice pay for major medical needs without relying on insurance. Your patients come to you for everyday needs. We help them pay for treatments outside of the scope of your practice. Our membership is a great fit for families who are generally healthy, but want to be prepared without paying overpriced insurance premiums.

By teaming up with us, your patients gain a financial safety net — and you offer a comprehensive healthcare solution.

Let's Talk

Greater peace of mind for you and your patients.

We team up with direct primary care providers to offer support to your members and promote loyalty to you practice. By offering a health share option, you can:

  • Add value to your membership by helping patients save money and gain peace of mind

  • Encourage preventive care by connecting patients with funds for preventive screenings

  • Connect with a community of DPC providers for collaboration and support

  • Benefit from an after-hours medical line for your DPC members.

We’re here to make life easier for you and your members.

How it works:

Members of Big Stuff Health Share make monthly contributions to a shared fund. They pay in cash for the care they need with assistance from the community for eligible expenses, up to $1 million per member.

We provide educational and marketing materials to help you communicate the program to members who are a fit. You’ll have our ongoing support and collaboration for your growing practice

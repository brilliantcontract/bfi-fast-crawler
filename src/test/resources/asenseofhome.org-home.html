Creating Homes.
Restoring Hope.
Preventing Homelessness.

1,200 Homes & Counting

At A Sense of Home, we believe that everyone deserves the dignity of a safe, fully furnished home. Our mission is to prevent homelessness by creating homes for displaced individuals and families, offering more than just furniture — we offer a fresh start.

Whether rebuilding after aging out of foster care or recovering from disaster, we provide essential home goods and furnishings through the power of community and volunteerism. Our work lays the foundation for healing, stability, and long-term success.

Celebrating
10 Years

From empty spaces to full hearts—10 years of changing lives, one home at a time.

Our Programs

Creating Homes for Former Foster Youth

Too many young adults age out of the foster care system without the support needed to thrive. Our Welcome Home program transforms empty spaces into fully furnished, functional, and beautiful homes for former foster youth — all with the help of generous donors and volunteers.

Disaster Relief for Survivors of LA Fires

In the wake of devastating fires across Los Angeles, we launched an emergency response program to help families recover and rebuild. We provide furnishings, essentials, and comfort to those who have lost everything — giving them the support they need to start again.

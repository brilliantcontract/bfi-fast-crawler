American Housing Foundation, Inc, a nationally recognized 501(c)(3), was formed in 1995, with the principles and goals of focusing on suitable locations to acquire, develop, design, construct, and manage affordable housing units for the elderly and low-income individuals and/or families.

The mission of American Housing Management Company, Inc. is to provide property management services in accordance with the principals and goals of American Housing Foundation, Inc.

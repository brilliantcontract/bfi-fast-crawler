WHAT IS AGFORESTRY LEADERSHIP?

Over 18 months, the AgForestry Leadership Program presents 11 in-state, plus one national, and one international seminar. All are intensive and hands-on learning experiences held at colleges, universities, businesses, organizations, as well as in the field, forest or stream.

For each leadership class, 18 to 24 candidates are chosen from a pool of state-wide applicants representing a range of professions and industries including: agriculture, forestry, and fishing (producers, processors, shippers, marketing and sales) and related fields. The selection process is highly competitive.  

WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

The AgForestry Leadership Program is the only one of its kind in Washington State that specifically focuses on developing adult leaders in natural resources. Collaborative leadership skills are needed now more than ever. The AgForestry Leadership Program shapes the future of natural resources by shaping future leaders. 

AgForestry cultivates leaders who communicate, collaborate, inspire, and serve.

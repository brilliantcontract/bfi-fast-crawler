A Premier Foster Family and Adoption Agency

Better Life Children Services is a Foster Family and Adoption Agency licensed by the State of California. Our professional staff are committed to helping children achieve permanence by engaging birth families, extended family members, community leaders and forever families who are prepared and willing to meet the needs of children who need out of home placement. Our goal is to place children in quality homes that provide healthy, safe, stable, and supportive environments, where family ties are nurtured and last forever. Find out more about Better Life.

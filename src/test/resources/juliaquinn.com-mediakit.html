Career Highlights ----------------- * ![Image 11](https://juliaquinn.com/WP/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/JQ_diaries_RITA-win-906x1024.jpg)Julia Quinn is the author of nineteen consecutive _New York Times_ bestsellers. In March 2021, all eight Bridgerton novels were on the _NYT_ list at the same time, a record (at that time) for an adult fiction author. * In the United States alone, there are over 20 million copies of her books in print. * On December 25, 2020, [Netflix premiered _BRIDGERTON_](https://juliaquinn.com/netflix/), based on her popular [series of novels](https://juliaquinn.com/series/bridgertons/) about the Bridgerton family. Season 2 premiered on March 25, 2022. Season 3 premiered on May 16, 2024. * Her social media is growing fast, with [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/juliaquinnauthor/) at 528K and [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/AuthorJuliaQuinn/) at 404K. * Her books have been translated into 43 languages, the most recent of which is Mongolian. * In 2016 she taught the inaugural romance workshop at the Yale Summer Writers Conference.

Bridgerton renewed for 2 more seasons!

May 14, 2025

*This post has been updated to reflect updated release dates for Bridgerton Special Editions

BIG SHOW NEWS! It’s official! Netflix has renewed Bridgerton for Seasons 5 and 6. This means two more Bridgerton siblings are guaranteed their love stories onscreen. No word yet on when those seasons will air, but we DO know that Season 4 will premiere in 2026. It’s still a long way away, so in the meantime, please enjoy this brand new clip of Benedict’s first sighting of Sophie. (And then backtrack to the beginning of the book JuliaQuinn.com’s Offer from a Gentleman excerpt will take you right up to that very moment when he first lays eyes upon her.)

GORGEOUS BOOK NEWS: the new Bridgerton Deluxe Collector’s set starts publishing on October 7, 2025 with The Duke and I, and continues with approximately one book per month.

These books are extra-special as it’s the first time JQ has had a hand in designing her own books. In addition to the beautiful William Morris inspired covers, each book contains a custom, full-color family tree, designed by JQ’s sister Emily (using art created by their late sister Violet).

Currently, only the first three volumes are available for preorder at most bookstores, but all eight can be ordered at JQ’s home bookstore, University Book Store in Seattle. All books ordered at UBS will be signed (and personalized, if you wish) in a color to match the book cover. Please note that you will receive your books after the LAST book that you order is published. In other words, if you order all eight books, you will receive them as a set when On the Way to the Wedding is released. If you want the books as they come out, you need to order them one at a time.

EVENTS ADDED! Don’t forget to regularly check JQ’s events calendar. She’ll be at the Tacoma Book Fair this coming weekend in Ruston, WA. And in June:Naples, Italy, and Cambridge, MA. Coming soon to herevents calendar: Boston, northern England, Copenhagen, and Gothenburg, Sweden.

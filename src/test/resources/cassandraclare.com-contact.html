Ask Cassandra

Hey! Drop your questions and thoughts for me (Cassie) here.  Edited to add: Just a few short notes on things I can’t help with:

  • Anything that has to do with TV or movies. Authors don’t make film or TV adaptations or have control over them, so I won’t be able to tell you anything about past adaptations or whether there will be any in future.
  • Signed books: For signed books, please contact Book Moon Books (for Shadowhunter books) or Odyssey Books (for Sword Catcher or international orders) where they can help you get signed and personalized copies mailed to you.
  • Reading any unasked for writing: including: novels, short stories, novellas, screenplays, poems, etc: Unfortunately for legal reasons, that’s a no can do.
  • Making birthday or congratulations videos: It’s my most common request, and I wish I had the time, but I just don’t!
  • Helping with school projects/theses/papers: Even if the papers are about me, I’m super flattered, but I’m backed up with requests for the next eight months. Only get in contact if you don’t mind waiting nine months for help or answers!
